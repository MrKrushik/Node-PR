About Flavora

Our Story

Welcome to Flavora, where culinary artistry meets warm hospitality. Since 2010, we've been crafting exceptional dining experiences that celebrate fresh ingredients and innovative flavors.


Founded by Chef Michael Anderson, Flavora started as a small family-owned restaurant with a dream to bring gourmet dining to the heart of Surat. Today, we're proud to serve over 500 guests daily, maintaining the same commitment to quality and excellence that started it all.


Our talented chefs blend traditional techniques with modern creativity to bring you dishes that not only taste amazing but look stunning too. Every plate is a masterpiece, crafted with locally-sourced ingredients and international spices.


We believe in sustainability, quality, and making every guest feel like family. Our restaurant features an open kitchen concept where you can watch our chefs in action, creating culinary magic right before your eyes.


Join us for an unforgettable dining journey that engages all your senses!
